About the game

Pixel Runner is our love letter to classic endless runners. With its minimalist pixel art style and responsive controls, it offers a challenging yet accessible experience for players of all skill levels.

The game features procedurally generated levels, meaning no two runs are ever the same. Master the rhythm of jumps, collect power-ups, and compete for the highest score on global leaderboards.

The story

In a world made entirely of pixels, you are a lone runner trying to escape the digital void. The world behind you is collapsing pixel by pixel, and your only chance is to keep running forward.

Along the way, you'll discover fragments of lost memories, each telling a story of the world that was. Can you run fast enough to reach the edge of existence?
