Across the Pages

Across the Pages

Narrative Adventure 2D Puzzle

A narrative-driven 2D adventure where you jump between storybook pages. Every page holds a new mystery, every chapter reveals a hidden truth.

The story

A forgotten Sharpener awakens in a silent attic and is pulled into the shimmering pages of a leather-bound sketchbook, a world of ink and ash where the sky is made of parchment.

As it navigates the ruins of a shattered canvas, the Sharpener discovers a hidden power to transform into a muscular form of pure light to battle the shadows watching its every move.

The journey becomes a race to find the Boy, the vanished artist whose hand once gave this world life, and to uncover why this crumbling masterpiece is tied to the Sharpener's own existence.why?

Features

4 Unique Worlds

Arts,Languages,Sciences, and Mathematics - each with distinct art and gameplay.

9 Unique Level

Each realm offers a new mechanic to master, moving from the weightless platforming of the Cloud City to the claustrophobic corridors of the Haunted Mansion.

Hand-drawn Art

Every page is beautifully illustrated in a unique artistic style.
